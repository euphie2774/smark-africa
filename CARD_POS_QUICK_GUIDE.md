# Smark-Africa Rewards Card - POS Quick Guide

## For Cashiers & POS Staff

---

## What is a Smark-Africa Rewards Card?

A physical card that customers can use to pay for purchases at Smark-Africa stores. Each card has:
- A unique barcode that you scan like a product
- A 4-digit PIN that the customer enters
- A balance of shopping credits and cash

**Important:** These cards ONLY work at Smark-Africa POS terminals. They are NOT bank cards or credit cards.

---

## How to Process a Card Payment (5 Easy Steps)

### Step 1: Add Products to Cart
- Scan all products as normal
- Verify quantities and prices

### Step 2: Scan the Customer's Card
- Ask customer for their Smark-Africa Rewards Card
- **Flip the card to the BACK side** (barcode is on the back)
- Use your barcode scanner to scan the barcode on the back of the card
- **OR** manually type the card number (on front) into the "Card Number" field

### Step 3: Verify Card Information
After scanning, you'll see:
- ✓ Customer name
- ✓ Card number (last 4 digits shown: •••• 1234)
- ✓ Available balance
- ✓ Card status (must say "Active")

**Auto-filled:** Customer email and phone will populate automatically

### Step 4: Select Payment Method
- In the "Payment Method" dropdown, select **"Smark-Africa Card"**
- System will automatically select this after you scan a card

### Step 5: Get Customer PIN
- Ask customer to **scratch the panel on their PIN slip** (if not done already)
- Customer enters their **4-digit PIN** in the PIN field
- Click **"Complete Sale"**

### Done! ✓
- System checks PIN and balance
- Deducts amount from card
- Prints receipt showing card transaction

## Common Scenarios

### ✅ Successful Payment
```
Customer balance: KSh 5,000
Purchase amount:  KSh 3,200
Result: Payment approved
New balance: KSh 1,800
```

### ❌ Insufficient Balance
```
Customer balance: KSh 1,500
Purchase amount:  KSh 3,200
Result: Payment declined - not enough balance
```

**Solution:** Offer split payment (see below)

### 🔒 Wrong PIN
```
Result: "Invalid PIN"
```

**Solution:**
- Ask customer to verify PIN on their scratch slip
- Customer may try again (3 attempts recommended)
- If still wrong, suggest they contact admin to reset

### 📵 Card Blocked or Lost
```
Result: "Card not recognized" or "Card inactive"
```

**Solution:**
- Politely inform customer their card cannot be used
- Ask them to visit admin desk or contact support
- Accept alternative payment method

## Split Payment (Card + Cash/Other)

If customer doesn't have enough balance on their card:

1. Select **"Split Payment"** from Payment Method dropdown
2. Enter amounts for each payment type:
   - **Card part:** How much from the card (up to available balance)
   - **Cash part:** Remaining amount in cash
   - **Mobile Money part:** Amount via M-Pesa/Mobile Money
3. Total of all parts must equal sale total
4. Scan card and get PIN as normal
5. Collect remaining amount via other methods
6. Complete sale

**Example:**
```
Sale Total: KSh 5,000
Card Balance: KSh 3,000

Split:
- Card: KSh 3,000
- Cash: KSh 2,000
Total: KSh 5,000 ✓
```

## Scanner Types Supported

### 📱 Phone Scanner
- Use your phone camera to scan barcodes
- Open the phone scanner link from POS screen
- Scan card barcode with phone
- Card details appear automatically on POS

### 🔌 USB Scanner (Wired)
- Plug scanner into USB port
- Click into the "Wired Scanner Input" field
- Scan card barcode
- Card details populate instantly

### 📶 Bluetooth Scanner
- Pair scanner with POS device
- Works same as USB scanner
- Scan directly into input field

### ⌨️ Manual Entry
- If scanner fails, type the card number manually
- Find card number printed on the card
- Enter in "Card Number" field
- Press Enter or Tab

## Troubleshooting

### Problem: Barcode won't scan
**Solutions:**
1. **Make sure you're scanning the BACK of the card** (barcode is on the back)
2. Try different angle or distance
3. Clean the scanner lens
4. Check if barcode is damaged
5. Manually type card number (shown on front of card)

### Problem: Card number entered but no info shows
**Solutions:**
1. Click the "Scan Card Barcode" button
2. Wait a few seconds for system to load
3. Check internet connection
4. Verify card number is correct

### Problem: Customer forgot PIN
**Solutions:**
1. Ask if they have the scratch slip
2. PIN is under the scratch-off panel
3. If lost, customer must visit admin desk to reset

### Problem: System says "Card not found"
**Solutions:**
1. Verify card number is correct (check with customer)
2. Card may not be issued yet
3. Card may be reported as lost/stolen
4. Ask customer to contact admin

## Important Reminders

### ✓ DO:
- Always ask customer to enter PIN themselves
- Check that card status shows "Active"
- Verify the balance covers the purchase
- Print receipt showing card transaction details
- Be polite if payment is declined

### ✗ DON'T:
- Never ask for or record customer PINs
- Don't accept cards that show "Blocked" or "Lost"
- Don't process without verifying customer identity
- Never force a transaction if PIN is wrong multiple times
- Don't share customer card information

## Customer Questions & Answers

**Q: "How do I check my card balance?"**  
A: When we scan your card, the balance shows on our screen. We can tell you before processing the payment.

**Q: "Can I use this card at other stores?"**  
A: No, this card only works at Smark-Africa POS terminals.

**Q: "I lost my PIN slip, can you tell me my PIN?"**  
A: For security, we cannot access your PIN. Please visit the admin desk to reset it.

**Q: "Can I get a refund to my card?"**  
A: Refunds are handled by the admin team. They can credit your card after processing the refund.

**Q: "How do I earn more credits?"**  
A: You earn credits automatically when you shop with us. The more you spend, the more credits you earn!

**Q: "My card is damaged, can I still use it?"**  
A: If the barcode is damaged, we can try typing the card number manually. If that doesn't work, you'll need a replacement card from admin.
