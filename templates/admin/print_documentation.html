7. User Guide: Shopping & Checkout

How to Shop on Smark-Africa

  1. Browse Products: Use the shop page to browse by category or search for specific items.
  2. Add to Cart: Click "Add to Cart" on any product you wish to purchase.
  3. Review Cart: Check your cart to adjust quantities or remove items.
  4. Checkout: Proceed to checkout, enter your shipping details.
  5. Payment: Complete payment via M-Pesa STK Push - you'll receive a prompt on your phone.
  6. Confirmation: Receive order confirmation and tracking details.
  7. Delivery: Track your order until it arrives at your doorstep.

Payment Methods

Currently, Smark-Africa supports M-Pesa (Lipa Na M-Pesa) as the primary payment method. The STK Push technology means you simply confirm the payment on your phone - no need to manually enter payment codes.

Delivery Options

  • Doorstep Delivery: Products delivered directly to your address
  • Pickup Station: Collect from a nearby pickup point

8. Shopping Cards & Loyalty Program

What is a Smark-Africa Shopping Card?

The Shopping Card is a physical loyalty card issued to customers who meet the minimum purchase threshold. It works like a rewards card - earn credits on every purchase and enjoy exclusive benefits.

How to Get a Card

  • Accumulate purchases totaling KSh {{ settings.get('shopping_card_min_purchase_kes', '10,000') }} or more
  • Once eligible, request your card from your account dashboard
  • A one-time card issuance fee of KSh {{ settings.get('shopping_card_issue_fee_kes', '700') }} applies
  • Your card will be delivered to your registered address

Card Benefits

  • Earn {{ settings.get('shopping_card_credits_per_100_kes', '1') }} credit(s) for every KSh 100 spent
  • Exclusive card-holder discounts and promotions
  • Priority customer support
  • Early access to flash sales and events

In Case of Card Loss

If you lose your Smark-Africa Shopping Card:

  1. Log into your account immediately
  2. Navigate to "My Card & Credits"
  3. Report the card as lost/stolen
  4. A replacement card will be issued (replacement fee applies)
  5. Your credits and history are safe - they are linked to your account, not the physical card

14. Debugging & Technical Notes

Platform Architecture

  • Backend: Python Flask with SQLAlchemy ORM
  • Database: SQLite (development) / PostgreSQL (production-ready)
  • Frontend: Bootstrap 5, Jinja2 Templates
  • Payment: Safaricom Daraja API (M-Pesa STK Push)
  • Email: Resend API with SMTP fallback
  • Hosting: Render.com with SSL
  • Security: Flask-Talisman, CSRF protection, rate limiting

Key Technical Decisions

  • Synchronous workers (not gevent) to avoid SSL recursion issues
  • HTTP API for Resend instead of SDK to avoid gevent conflicts
  • Shopping card numbers use prefix {{ settings.get('shopping_card_prefix', '607845') }} for brand identification
  • Market price caching with configurable refresh intervals
  • Background job scheduling with APScheduler
