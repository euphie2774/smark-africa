Barcode Scanning

Each card includes a unique barcode that can be scanned at POS terminals using phone cameras, USB scanners, or Bluetooth barcode readers.

Card Usage

Cards work only at Smark-Africa POS terminals. Customers scan the card barcode and enter their 4-digit PIN to authorize purchases.

Earning Credits
