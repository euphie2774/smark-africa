How It Works
PIN Setup (During Card Creation)
  1. Register: Apply for your Smark-Africa Rewards Card above
  2. Receive SMS: You'll get an SMS with a link to set your PIN
  3. Create Your PIN: Choose your own secure 4-digit PIN
  4. Card Activated: Once PIN is set, your card is active and ready to use
Using Your Card at POS
  1. Present Card: Show your Smark-Africa Rewards Card at the POS terminal
  2. Barcode Scanned: The cashier scans the barcode on the back of your card
  3. SMS Authorization: You receive an SMS prompt to authorize the payment amount
  4. Approve Payment: Approve or decline the transaction from your phone
  5. Done: Once approved, the amount is deducted from your card balance
Double Security: Your card is protected by a physical barcode AND mobile SMS authorization. Only you can approve payments from your registered phone.
